The Story of Roberts Schools

Incorporated on 25 July 2018 under registration RC 2633098, Roberts Schools was built with a vision of bridging the gap between local standards and high-fidelity global preparatory education. Over the years, we have grown from a local boutique provider into a premier icon of academic excellence inside Satellite Town, Lagos, transforming young lives daily.
